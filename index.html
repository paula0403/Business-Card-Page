About me

I am Paulina. I am a huge fan of handmade: knitting and sewing. This way I can relax and let my creativity run free. I get inspiration from cartoons and movies. I have completed the A.12 tailoring course.More of my projects and inspiration can be found on the blog. Apart from handmade, my passion is traveling in southern Europe. I love extreme mountain tourism, especially in the Polish Tatras, for example Eagle Path. I'm crazy about Spain and spanish culture and language. I have been learning web programming for several months: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript. I would like to change my career path in the future. My projects

Thick woolen sweater for bitter frosts. In the pattern I used nups (balls) and a honeycomb motif. In general, the sweater is quite rich in various motifs. I do not hide that it was laborious and time-consuming. I made the front and back in the same way, the only difference is the presence of the balls, which would not hurt the back;). The sleeves also feature a honeycomb pattern. To liven up the whole piece of knitting the head of the sleeve has been made with a lighter yarn. The whole thing, of course, is high in wool (70% and 50%). In fact, the mere sight of it makes me warm.

A sweater made in the form of a circle. Usually it is called the turner, but mine reminds me more of the wheel because of its stiffness. It is made of a mixture of wool and cashmere with a bright cotton-wool finish to illuminate the whole. I like the pattern very much and I'm about to make a mini and summer cotton version.

Romantic set. The semicircle skirt is made of thick dove-colored neoprene. It is very light and comfortable. It has a metal zipper sewn in at the back, which gives it a bit of a vanguard character. For the skirt I have completed a sweater / dress crochet with a shell pattern. I used plum Yarn Art. Kid Mohair with a lot of mohair to make it. It is very light, stylish and soft to the touch. It looks great with an elegant shirt.

A christening set for a boy. It consists of a fluffy jacket with a hood made of shiny polyamide yarn. Soft bamboo sweater has front elements designed on the basis of semicircles. A "baseball" cap made of acrylic yarn with a golden thread. The last element are thick cotton sneakers that give the set a casual character. The whole is complemented by navy blue and blue accessories: buttons, bows, laces.

The airy scarf made just on request. It is intended to serve as an undersuit in case of cold weather. I used a yarn with a lot of mohair in a light gray color. Finally, the dimensions were 130cmx60cm, so the size to "wrap around" is ok. The pattern consists of leaf motifs, which makes it incredibly light. The scarf is very warm and airy at the same time.
